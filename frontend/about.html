About Hotel Navjeevan Palace

Your Comfortable Stay in the Heart of Udaipur

Welcome to Hotel Navjeevan Palace

The legacy of Hotel Navjeevan began in 1971 with our original and well-established Surajpole branch in Udaipur. Built on decades of trust and guest satisfaction, Hotel Navjeevan Palace is our new branch, created to continue the same tradition of hospitality while catering to the modern needs of today’s travelers.

Hotel Navjeevan Palace is a budget-friendly hotel located in the city of Udaipur, Rajasthan, offering comfortable and reliable accommodation for travelers seeking an affordable yet pleasant stay in the City of Lakes.

Strategically located on City Station Road, just minutes away from Udaipur Railway Station as well as Roadways Bus Stand, our hotel provides easy access to major tourist attractions including the City Palace, Lake Pichola, Jagdish Temple, and other popular destinations.

With over 54 years of excellence in hospitality, our commitment remains unchanged — to provide clean, comfortable rooms, courteous service, and essential modern amenities at honest and affordable prices. We take pride in offering a welcoming environment that suits families, solo travelers, business guests, and groups alike.

At Hotel Navjeevan Palace, we believe in comfort without complexity, service with sincerity, and value you can trust. We look forward to welcoming you and making your stay in Udaipur comfortable, convenient, and memorable.
