About Hotel Navjeevan Palace

Your Comfortable Stay in the Heart of Udaipur

Welcome to Hotel Navjeevan Palace

Hotel Navjeevan Palace is a budget-friendly hotel located in the heart of Udaipur, Rajasthan. We offer comfortable accommodation for travelers seeking an affordable yet pleasant stay in the City of Lakes.

Strategically located on City Station Road, just minutes away from Udaipur Railway Station, our hotel provides easy access to major tourist attractions including the City Palace, Lake Pichola, Jagdish Temple, and many more.

Our commitment is to provide clean, comfortable rooms with modern amenities at affordable prices, making us an ideal choice for families, solo travelers, and groups visiting Udaipur.

Prime Location

Located on City Station Road in Shivaji Nagar, we are just a short walk from Udaipur Railway Station and easily accessible from all parts of the city.

Budget-Friendly

We offer competitive rates starting from ₹999 per night, making quality accommodation accessible to all travelers without compromising on comfort.

Comfortable Rooms

Our rooms are well-maintained and equipped with essential amenities including AC, TV, WiFi, attached bathrooms, and 24/7 hot water supply.

Quality Service

Our friendly staff is committed to ensuring your stay is comfortable and memorable. We offer room service, housekeeping, and assistance with local information.
